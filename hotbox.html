Hotbox Escape Rooms

PURPOSE

Hot Box Escape Rooms is dedicated to offering unique, premium escape rooms with intoxicating thrills and team-building opportunities. They promise their customers an unforgettable experience that will leave them with smiles, pounding hearts, and stories to share. They are looking for a visual identity that aligns with their mission, as well as cohesive print and digital collateral to entice viewers to visit. The $5 off promotion will entice viewers to sign up for their first room. The needs of the target audience include having a great, memorable experience. The challenge will be enticing them to come back again.

PROCESS

The brand strategy focuses on exciting the audience with bold, captivating, and intriguing designs.

  • The dark colors paired with the vibrant hot red create an engaging and thrilling look that will excite viewers.
  • The sans-serif typography creates a modern and bold look that grabs viewers’ attention.
  • “Looking for a thrill this Friday night?” and “The best hour of your night” will resonate with the audience who are looking for new, exciting options for a night out.

Designer roles: logo design & visual identity, brand strategy, and print and digital collateral utilizing Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and After Effects.
