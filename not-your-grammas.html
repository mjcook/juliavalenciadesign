Not Your Gramma’s

PURPOSE

Not Your Gramma’s is committed to offering sustainable, eco-friendly soap at an affordable price. Their goal is to market the Head to Toe Body Bar to a younger, eco-conscious audience who will become lifelong customers. They are looking for a brand identity that aligns with their mission and sets them apart from the competition, as well as cohesive print and digital collateral to promote the product and its promotions.

PROCESS

The strategy of this project is to communicate the brand’s message of being a soap that does it all– cleans from head to toe, is sustainable, environmentally friendly, and nourishing. Eco-friendly soap competitors were researched to help develop a visual identity that will help Not Your Gramma’s shine from the competition.

The overall brand style is modern, playful, and eco-conscious.

  • The uplifting and positive colors help the designs pop and excite the audience. Purple conveys luxury for this premium soap that is nourishing and effective. Yellow energizes the designs and guides the viewers’ eyes to important elements. Green is used sparingly to illustrate the brand as sustainable and eco-friendly.
  • The sans serif typography paired with the complementary serif typeface create a modern look and feel.
  • The imagery and photography style allow the product to shine and add vibrancy to the campaign. The purple gradient helps the designs pop.
  • “Good for You. Good for Earth” and “Proudly Made” appeal to the viewer who wants a soap that is healthy and nourishing for their body, but is also kind to the Earth.

Designer roles: logo design & visual identity, brand strategy, package design, print and digital collateral utilizing Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Premiere Pro, and After Effects.

University Course: Senior Design Capstone
