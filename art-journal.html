Art Journal Video

PURPOSE

The goal was to plan, film, edit, and produce a short, inspirational how-to video. My vision was to create an uplifting, easy-to-follow video that inspires people to try art journaling, one of my favorite activities to express creativity freely.

PROCESS

A video shot list including shot types, camera angles, camera movements, and shot descriptions, was planned and organized to guide the filming process. The filming aimed to capture stunning compositions and engaging visuals for the viewer. The footage was logged, imported, organized, and then edited with music and graphics.

Designer roles: Video shooting, editing, and production utilizing Premiere Pro.

University Course: Digital Video Production
