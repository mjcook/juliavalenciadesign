Brookgreen Gardens Commercial

PURPOSE

The goal was to capture the beautiful charm of Brookgreen and showcase its lovely attractions and activities that will entice viewers to visit.

PROCESS

A shot list and voice-over script were planned and organized to guide production. The filming focused on capturing engaging, appealing compositions and shots that interest the audience. The music, voice-over, and pace of the video highlight the peacefulness of Brookgreen the viewer will experience when visiting.

Designer roles: Video shooting, editing, and production utilizing Premiere Pro.

University Course: Multimedia Techniques
