Hawks & Reed Posters

PURPOSE

Hawks & Reed Performing Arts Center is a multi-venue performance center that is passionate about bringing art and music to its community. The goal of this graphic design internship was to create engaging and captivating posters for a variety of events to promote ticket sales and spread brand awareness.

PROCESS

  • Conceptualized and designed posters with dynamic and varying styles to match the musical genre or mood of the event.
  • Designed engaging visuals and compositions by creating and combining photographs, vector graphics, captivating colors, and striking typography.

Designer roles: Conceptualization and poster design utilizing Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.
